Background

同事们，大家好：

人工智能技术迭代速度远超预期，早已从单纯的工作辅助工具，升级为全新的生产方式。从内容创作、代码开发，到复杂业务决策支持，AI已经开始进入越来越多关键环节，正在快速向企业内部渗透。

对企业来说，AI带来的不只是工作效率提升，更意味着组织运行方式、决策逻辑乃至人才结构都在被重新定义。以往依靠经验积累、层级传递和人力规模的传统运作模式，正在被更高效、更直接的方式所替代。

在一些已经展开实践的场景中，这种变化正在变得具体而清晰：小规模团队借助AI，就能完成过去需要多倍人力投入才能做的方案设计、数据分析甚至客户解决方案输出，以往企业靠“人力规模”和“经验沉淀”为核心的优势正在被快速削弱；决策过程中，大量中间分析与汇总环节被压缩，信息从产生到形成决策建议的路径被缩短，现有的层级管理模式也需要重新审视；在部分业务模式中，产品和服务的边界正在被打破，企业能用更低成本、更快速度，提供过去需要多部门复杂协同才能完成的综合解决方案。

这些变化不只是局部工作优化，更会带来深层次的全面重塑。包括商业模式的重构、组织形态与架构的简化或重组，也会重新定义“人“在组织中的角色与价值。

这意味着，组织面对的已经不是“是否使用AI”的选择，而是在新一轮以AI为核心的生产方式迭代背景下“是否还具备持续运作与竞争的能力”。如果不能主动调整和提升自身能力，原有的组织优势很可能在短时间内被削弱。

过往我们一贯的选择，是在关键变化面前不观望、不等待，主动站到前沿，推动变革发生。面对AI带来的这一轮变化，我们同样需要以更主动、更前瞻的方式，推动组织的持续进化。

因此，公司决定面向内外部招聘人员组建团队，牵头推动公司内部AI变革。团队既要探索AI的应用潜力，也要创造实际价值，通过AI整合优化核心业务流程、落地适配组织内部工作场景的AI应用和智能体，系统推动关键业务场景与管理流程的重构。

Dear Colleagues,

The iteration speed of AI technology has far exceeded expectations, upgrading from a mere work-assist tool to an entirely new mode of production. From content creation and code development to complex business decision support, AI is rapidly penetrating key enterprise processes.

For enterprises, AI brings more than just efficiency; it redefines operational methods, decision-making logic, and talent structures. The traditional operational model relying on experience accumulation, hierarchical transmission, and scale of manpower is being replaced by more efficient and direct methods.

In practical scenarios, this change is clear: small teams using AI can complete designs and analyses that previously required multiple times the manpower, rapidly weakening traditional advantages based on "manpower scale" and "experience." Decision-making paths are compressed, and existing hierarchical management models need re-examination. The boundaries of products and services are breaking down, allowing enterprises to provide comprehensive solutions faster and at lower costs.

These changes will bring deep, comprehensive reshaping, including business model reconstruction, organizational simplification, and redefining the role of "humans" in the organization.

This means the organization is no longer facing a choice of "whether to use AI," but rather "whether it still has the capability to operate and compete." If we do not proactively adjust, our existing advantages may quickly diminish.

Our consistent choice is not to wait, but to stand at the forefront and drive transformation. Facing this AI revolution, we must drive continuous evolution proactively.

Therefore, the company has decided to recruit internally and externally to form a team to lead internal AI transformation. The team will explore AI potential, create practical value, integrate core business workflows, and implement AI agents suited for internal scenarios to systematically drive the reconstruction of key business and management processes.

I. Department Mission
]

该团队将围绕以下核心使命开展工作：

The team will focus on the following core missions:

  • 1. 探索与验证AI在组织内的落地路径：系统评估AI技术在不同业务与管理场景中的应用可能性，以AI化手段直接切入并解决组织运行中的关键痛点，逐步形成新的组织能力优势。
  • 2. 推动核心业务流程的重构与再设计：跳出既有流程与方法论约束，以AI能力为基础，对核心业务流进行重新设计与整合，实现跨越式效率提升与流程再造，而非局部优化。
  • 3. 构建面向AI时代的组织形态与工作机制：探索并建立新型组织机制，引入智能体、数字员工等形态，重构人与系统的协作方式，形成可持续演进的智能化工作体系。
  • 4. 推动AI在公司内部的深度融合与日常化应用：将AI能力嵌入日常工作与管理场景，推动从“个别使用”向“广泛协同”的转变，逐步形成AI驱动的内部生态。
  • 1. Explore and verify AI implementation paths: Systematically evaluate AI tech in business scenarios to solve key operational pain points directly, gradually forming new organizational advantages.
  • 2. Reconstruct and redesign core workflows: Break free from existing constraints. Redesign core flows based on AI capabilities for exponential efficiency gains, not just local optimization.
  • 3. Build AI-era organizational mechanisms: Establish new mechanisms, introduce AI agents and digital employees, reconstruct human-system collaboration, and form a sustainable intelligent work system.
  • 4. Promote deep AI integration daily: Embed AI capabilities into daily management, shifting from "individual use" to "widespread synergy," forming an AI-driven internal ecosystem.

Now, the company is calling for volunteer applications from all internal employees. After passing the interview assessment, you will join the team to achieve goals alongside externally recruited experts and professionals.
